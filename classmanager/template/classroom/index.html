About Us

📚 Class Manager is your all-in-one student-teacher portal, bringing the classroom online with seamless, interactive features.

👩‍🏫 Interactive Learning: Students and teachers can log in to access a dynamic digital classroom, fostering collaboration and engagement.

📊 Powerful Student Management: Teachers can effortlessly organize classes, track student progress, and keep everyone updated with real-time notifications.

  • 📤 Upload and submit assignments with ease.
  • 📢 Share class notices instantly.
  • 💬 Communicate directly through in-portal messaging.
  • 📈 Monitor grades and performance with smart analytics.

Our Services

For Teacher

  1. Teachers can add students to their class.
  2. Give or edit student marks.
  3. Post class notices for all students.
  4. Upload assignments for students.
  5. Check submitted assignments and grade them.
  6. Receive messages from students in Inbox.
  7. View and edit marks from a student’s profile.
  8. Upload a profile picture in Edit Profile.

For Student

  1. Students can login in the portal and keep themselves updated of all the activities going on in the class.
  2. Students can see the notice given by their teacher in the Class Notice option.
  3. Students can download their assignments in the Assignments option and can also submit it their. ( Once assignment is submitted, it can't be changed later. )
  4. Students can contact any teacher through message option in All Teachers List.
  5. Students can check their marks in the Marks option.
  6. User can add profile pic through edit profile option.
